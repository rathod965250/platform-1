## How to Fix Common Issues

### Camera Permission Denied
1. Click the camera icon in your browser's address bar
2. Select "Allow" for camera access
3. Click the "Refresh" button in System Check

### Microphone Permission Denied
1. Click the microphone icon in your browser's address bar
2. Select "Allow" for microphone access
3. Click the "Refresh" button in System Check

### Slow Internet Connection
1. Close unnecessary browser tabs
2. Disconnect other devices from WiFi
3. Move closer to your WiFi router
4. Consider using ethernet cable

### Low Battery
1. Connect your charger immediately
2. Close unnecessary applications
3. Reduce screen brightness

### No Camera/Microphone Found
1. Check if device is properly connected
2. Try unplugging and reconnecting
3. Restart your browser
4. Check device drivers (Windows: Device Manager)

## Manual Refresh

If any check shows outdated information:
1. Click the "Refresh" button at the top of System Check
2. All checks will run again
3. Wait a few seconds for results

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to allow camera access for all tests?
**A**: Only for proctored tests. However, it's good to verify it works beforehand.

### Q: Why does the internet check take a few seconds?
**A**: It measures your actual download speed by downloading a small test file.

### Q: Can I proceed if battery check shows "N/A"?
**A**: Yes! This is normal for desktop computers without batteries.

### Q: What if my internet speed is below 1 Mbps?
**A**: You can still proceed, but you may experience slower loading times.

### Q: Does the system check record me?
**A**: No! It only verifies that your camera and microphone work. No recording happens during the check.

### Q: How often does it check my internet?
**A**: Automatically every 30 seconds, plus whenever you click "Refresh".

### Q: What browsers are supported?
**A**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

### Q: Can I skip the system check?
**A**: The check runs automatically but doesn't prevent you from starting the test. However, we strongly recommend resolving any issues first.

### Q: Why do I see warnings even though everything works?
**A**: Warnings indicate sub-optimal conditions. You can proceed, but fixing them improves your test experience.

- ‚úÖ All checks run locally on your device
- ‚úÖ No data is sent to servers
- ‚úÖ Camera/microphone are never recorded during checks
- ‚úÖ Permissions can be revoked at any time

## Tips for Best Performance

### Before the Test
1. Run System Check at least 5 minutes before test time
2. Fix any warnings or errors
3. Close all unnecessary applications
4. Ensure stable power supply

### During the Test
1. Don't close the browser tab
2. Don't switch to other applications
3. Keep your device plugged in
4. Stay connected to WiFi

### After the Test
1. You can revoke camera/microphone permissions if desired
2. System Check results are not stored
